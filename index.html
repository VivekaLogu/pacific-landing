About US

Pacific DeFi aims to create a new financial ecosystem that unifies the current scattered DeFi landscape.

This includes saving, lending & borrowing, insurance, asset management, crypto banking, synthetic products & leveraged finance.

In order to provide the services above, Pacific DeFi will offer a universally usable wallet, token swap DEX platform, IDO platform, and inter-Blockchain liquidity transfer.

Mission

Pacific DeFi’s mission is to provide an honest and fully transparent, & decentralized, user-friendly platform.

It is in our belief that people should be in charge of their own financial destiny and not be at the whim of traditional financial institutions or governments. We want our token-holders to reap the rewards of this new financial revolution.

Liquidity Pool

Liquidity pools are one of the foundational technologies behind the current DeFi ecosystem.

They are an essential part of automated market makers (AMM), borrow-lend protocols, yield farming, synthetic assets, on-chain insurance, blockchain gaming – the list goes on.

In itself, the idea is profoundly simple. A liquidity pool is basically funds thrown together in a big digital pile. But what can you do with this pile in a permissionless environment.
